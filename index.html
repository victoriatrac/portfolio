Biography

Hi! I'm Victoria Trac, a technically-brained multi-disciplinary creator with a knack for creative problem solving. I have an insatiable desire to learn and over the last decade+ have found myself working on a variety of projects within the design spectrum. My intention is to create things that matter -- whether they evoke emotion or enhance experiences. I'm passionate about programming, creating tools that add efficiency, and designing immersive experiences.


I'm looking for a full-time career as a developer. I'm also open to 3D design opportunities, particularly related to modeling, lighting, and technical art. Currently, I'm freelancing while enrolled in Lambda School's full-stack web development program in order to further hone my programming skills. Prior to this, I worked as an interior designer, with over 200 spaces created in just over a year.


During my free time, I can be found cooking, spending time outdoors, and painting.


If you're looking for a self-motivated jack-of-all-trades with strong interpersonal skills and proven research prowess, I'd love to talk to you!
